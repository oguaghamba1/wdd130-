My Favorite City

My favorite city is Owerri, Imo state in Nigeria. It's where we lived before moving to the Uk. Wonderful people and very welcoming. Most of my family members still live there up till today. We had a company called Snow White Bottling Company that produces 18.9 ltr bottled water for dispenser machines. We lived at Federal Housing Estate, Egbeada, in an 8 bedroom duplex we built.
